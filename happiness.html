Welcome to HAPPYVERSE!

A beautiful board game designed to reduce stress, deepen connections, and discover what truly makes you happy. Through mindful challenges, joyful tasks, and meaningful reflections, you'll explore happiness alongside your loved ones.

How to Play

  • Choose 2-4 players and set your names
  • Take turns rolling the dice and moving around the board
  • Complete joyful tasks, mindful exercises, and connection challenges
  • Watch your colorful pawn move around the board!
  • Earn Happiness Points (HP) by participating fully
  • The player with the most HP wins, but everyone wins in joy! 💖
